Personality Test

Below are Traits of four personality types L,O,G,B respectively. For each group of traits select the number "4" next to the word that best describes you; number "3" next to the word that describes you next best; "2" for next best and "1" by the word that least describes you. In end on each group of words you will have one "4", one "3", one "2" and one "1".

For example: One choice for the first line of words would be as follows:
Likes Authority: 3, Enthusiastic: 4, Sensitive Feelings: 2, Likes Instructions: 1

How this works? L,O,G,B stands for 4 personality types Lions, Otters, Golden Retrievers and Beavers respectively. By adding the number for each trait, personality score is obtained. Personality type with highest scores is your dominant personality type. While you are a combinantion of all four personality types, but the highest scores reveal the most accurate picture of your natural inclinations, strengths and weaknesses, and how you will naturally respond in most situations

The four personality types are linked to animals to make them easier to understand and remember
